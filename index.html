Welcome to NearHear

Discover music online. See it nearby.

NearHear allows you to create Spotify playlists of artists with upcoming performances in your area. Listen to old favorites or discover new ones, then go see them live. Just specify a city and date range then listen in Spotify. Check the playlist description for a link to event information. Launching post-Covid. Enter your email and city here to be notified when we launch.
